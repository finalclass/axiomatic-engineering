## Visual inspiration

> A minimalist, futuristic task list interface floating in soft ambient light. A glassmorphism-style transparent card with subtle neon edges in shades of blue and lavender, displaying a few items with checkboxes connected by thin geometric lines. The background is a gentle dark gradient with faint grid lines receding into depth, evoking a calm digital space. Small luminous particles drift around the card. Clean sans-serif typography. Muted palette: deep navy, soft violet, ice-blue accents.

- **glassmorphism** — transparent cards with background blur
- **subtle neons** — futuristic accent without aggression (ice blue, soft violet)
- **dark gradient with perspective grid** — depth and modernity
- **fine particles** — lightness and movement
- **color palette** — deep navy, soft violet, ice-blue accents
